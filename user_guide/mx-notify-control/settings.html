Member Validation

Send additional information for users who pass "Self-activation via email" Special tags:
{mbr_username}, {mbr_screen_name}, {mbr_email}, {site_name}, {site_url}, {sender_id}, {member_id}

CP Member Activation

Say "Hello" to member if you have "Manual activation by an administrator" for new users Special tags:
{mbr_username}, {mbr_screen_name}, {mbr_email}, {site_name}, {site_url}, {sender_id}, {member_id}

Custom list

with :mx_notify_control:form you can create flexible subscribtion lists. For example you can give you user option to subscribe to entry_update notification. Special tags: 
{entry_id}
Example for subscribe form 
{exp:mx_notify_control:form result_page="subscribe_template" list_name=" entry_{entry_id}"}

<p><input type="text" name="email" value="{email}" /></p>

<p><input type="text" name="name" value="" /></p> <p>

Custom field

Used for Entry update & New Entry create triggers only. [b]MX Delegate field[/b] - List of person who will be in the list is entered in field settings. Message will be sended in case if entry will be updated and editor is chooses another person than was with previous entry update. [b]VMG Chosen Member[b] - Message will be sent to members which you choose in entry.
