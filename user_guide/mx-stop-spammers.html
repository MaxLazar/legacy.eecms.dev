MX Stop Spammers User Guide¶

Save your time, ban spammers easily! This module helps to find out the spammer easily by special member’s filters. Module also has good integration with Stop Forum Spam DB, so you can block spammer automatically during the registration, check members from your CP or post your own spammer's list to SFS DB.

  • One Click Ban
  • Integration with Stop Forum Spam DB
  • Trusted Members List
  • Check members with Stop Forum Spam DB during registration.
  • Easy member’s management with "potential spammers flag" filters
