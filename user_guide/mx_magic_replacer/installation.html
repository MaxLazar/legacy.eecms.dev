Installation

  1. Download the latest version of MX Magic Replacer and extract the .zip to your desktop
  2. Copy system/expressionengine/third_party/mx_magic_replacer to system/expressionengine/third_party/
  3. Enable the MX Magic Replacer extension in your CP
